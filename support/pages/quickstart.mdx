# Quickstart

## 1. Create an account

Sign in at [ba6-bsky-suite.com](https://ba6-bsky-suite.com) and create your workspace.

## 2. Connect a Bluesky account

Go to **Accounts** and connect your Bluesky handle with an app password.

## 3. Create a draft

Open **Drafts** to write post copy and save drafts for later.

## 4. Schedule a post

Select a draft, pick a time, and schedule. The queue will process automatically.

## 5. Review activity

Use **Overview** and **Events** to monitor execution status and recent activity.
