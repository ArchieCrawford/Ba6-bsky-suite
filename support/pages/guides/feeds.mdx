# Feeds

## Create a feed

1. Open **Feeds** in the control panel.
2. Choose a slug and title.
3. Enable or disable the feed as needed.

## Rules and sources

- Add include and exclude keywords to shape the content.
- Add account lists or sources to scope the feed.

## Publishing

Feeds become available once enabled. Use the feed URL in Bluesky clients.
