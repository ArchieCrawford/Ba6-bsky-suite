# Privacy Policy

**BA6 Bluesky Suite**  
*Last updated: January 29, 2026*

## 1. Information We Collect

BA6 collects only what is necessary to operate the service.

### A) Account Information

- Bluesky account identifiers (DID, handle)
- Authentication metadata required to perform scheduled actions

### B) Indexed Content

BA6 may store:

- Public post URIs
- Public post text
- Timestamps
- Author DIDs
- Language metadata

BA6 does **not** store private messages or restricted content.

---

## 2. How Data Is Used

Collected data is used solely to:

- Generate custom feeds
- Schedule and publish posts
- Maintain system reliability
- Improve performance and debugging

---

## 3. Data Retention

- Indexed data may be retained long‑term for feed operation, analysis, or archival purposes
- Older data may be archived to reduce system load
- Data is not sold or shared with third parties

---

## 4. Public Nature of Data

All indexed content originates from **public Bluesky posts**.

Users should understand:

- Public posts may be indexed and referenced
- BA6 does not alter or republish content outside Bluesky’s hydration process

---

## 5. User Requests

Users may request:

- Feed deletion
- Account data removal
- Clarification on stored data

Requests can be sent to: **[archie@ba6-bsky-suite.com](mailto:archie@ba6-bsky-suite.com)**

---

## 6. Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data. However, no system is perfectly secure.

## 7. Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect system changes or legal requirements.
